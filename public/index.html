What is Skillmatch?

SkillMatch is a collaboration tool that connects you to developers with the skills you need for any given project. Simply enter the technical skills you require and SkillMatch finds you people the right people for the job.

Advanced Algorithms

SkillMatch uses the most advanced algorithms taught in CMPE130 to provide you with the most optimal results. This website implements hashing, sorting, and graph theory.
